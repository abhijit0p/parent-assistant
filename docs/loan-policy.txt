Education Financing and Loan Policy

To improve accessibility, the academy may offer financing options through approved Non-Banking Financial Companies (NBFCs).

Loan Eligibility

Loan approval is based on multiple factors including:

- Parent credit score
- Income verification
- Historical payment behavior
- Internal risk assessment performed by the NBFC

Loan Approval

Loan approval is solely at the discretion of the financing NBFC.

The academy does not guarantee loan approval and has no authority to override the lender's decision.

Disbursement

Upon loan approval, the loan amount is paid directly by the NBFC to the academy.

The student subscription is activated only after successful loan disbursement.

Repayment

Loan repayment obligations exist solely between the parent and the NBFC.

EMIs are typically collected through bank auto-debit, NACH mandate, UPI AutoPay, or other repayment mechanisms approved by the lender.

Refund Restrictions

Subscriptions funded through loans are not eligible for refunds under any circumstances.

Cancellation of a subscription does not automatically cancel the loan agreement.

Dispute Resolution

Any dispute regarding loan approval, EMI schedules, interest calculations, penalties, or repayment terms must be addressed directly with the NBFC.

Illustrative Interest Rate

Interest rates may vary based on risk profile and lender policies. Typical annual interest rates may start at 10% per annum and may be higher depending on borrower eligibility.

The academy acts only as a facilitator and is not a party to the financing agreement between the parent and the NBFC.
