Refund Policy

The academy offers a limited refund policy for active subscriptions.

Refund requests must be submitted in writing through email or the academy support desk.

Refund eligibility is determined based on the number of classes attended under the subscription.

Refund Schedule

- If a refund is requested after attending 1 class, 100% of the subscription amount is refundable.
- If a refund is requested after attending 2 classes, 85% of the subscription amount is refundable.
- If a refund is requested after attending 3 classes, 75% of the subscription amount is refundable.
- After the first 3 classes, refunds are calculated on a prorated basis based on unused classes.

Maximum Refund Limit

Regardless of the number of unused classes remaining, the refund amount after the first 3 attended classes shall never exceed 50% of the original subscription value.

Non-Refundable Charges

Registration fees, admission fees, assessment fees, and third-party payment gateway charges are non-refundable.

Loan-Funded Subscriptions

Subscriptions that have been paid through an academy-approved loan program are not eligible for refunds under any circumstances.

Refund Processing

Approved refunds are generally processed within 7 to 14 business days and are returned to the original payment method whenever possible.

The academy reserves the right to request additional information before approving a refund.
