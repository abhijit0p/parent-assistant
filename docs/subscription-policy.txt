Subscription Modification Policy

The academy offers multiple subscription plans including monthly, quarterly, semester, annual, and package-based plans.

Plan Changes

Students may upgrade or downgrade their subscription plan only at the time of a payment event.

Examples include:

- Renewal payment
- Package recharge
- Subscription extension
- Annual plan conversion

Mid-Cycle Changes

Subscription changes are not permitted during an active billing cycle.

Any requested change will become effective only when the next payment is processed.

Pricing Impact

Changing a subscription may result in different pricing, discounts, package validity periods, and attendance limits.

Promotional Offers

Promotional pricing and discounts are subject to the terms applicable at the time of the payment transaction.

The academy reserves the right to discontinue or modify subscription plans without prior notice.
