Missed Class Make-Up Policy

The academy understands that students may occasionally miss scheduled classes.

Students may request make-up classes for missed sessions subject to the following conditions.

Eligibility

- Make-up classes are only available for classes missed during the current calendar month.
- Requests must be submitted before the end of the same month.
- A student may attend a maximum of 2 make-up classes per month.

Availability

Make-up classes are subject to coach availability, classroom capacity, and schedule compatibility.

The academy cannot guarantee a specific coach, batch, timing, or location for a make-up class.

Unused Make-Ups

Unused make-up opportunities cannot be carried forward to future months.

Missed make-up classes are treated as consumed and cannot be rescheduled again.

Non-Transferability

Make-up classes are specific to the enrolled student and cannot be transferred to siblings, friends, or other students.

Exceptional Circumstances

The academy may approve additional make-up classes in exceptional circumstances at its sole discretion.
